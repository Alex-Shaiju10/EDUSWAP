The Problem

Students want to learn.
The system makes it hard.

Traditional platforms fail college students in three big ways.

💸

Too Expensive

Quality courses on Udemy or Coursera can cost thousands of rupees — way out of reach for most students on tight budgets.

😴

Boring Lectures

One-way video lectures don't keep you engaged. You need real conversations, real feedback, and hands-on practice.

🔗

No Community

Learning alone is lonely. Students miss out on collaboration, networking, and finding peers who share their interests.

How It Works

Three steps to start
learning for free

EduSwap is built around a simple idea: everyone knows something worth teaching.

01

Create Your Profile

List the skills you have and the skills you want to learn. Your profile becomes your live skill portfolio.

02

Find a Match

Our algorithm connects you with students who need what you offer — and offer what you need. Mutual benefit, zero cost.

03

Start Swapping

Schedule live interactive sessions, learn from each other, earn badges, and grow your verified skill portfolio.

04

Build Your Network

Connect with a growing community of motivated students. Open doors to collaborations, projects, and opportunities.

Features

Everything you need
to learn smarter

Built specifically for college students who want real, practical learning without the price tag.

🔄

Skill Exchange Engine

Smart matching connects you with the right peers. Teach Python, learn design. Teach communication, learn data analysis.

🎥

Live Interactive Sessions

Real-time sessions with screen sharing, whiteboards, and chat. No pre-recorded lectures — just real conversations.

🏆

Verified Skill Portfolio

Earn badges and endorsements from peers. Build a portfolio that actually proves your skills to future employers.

🤝

Community & Clubs

Join interest-based groups, find project collaborators, and build your student network beyond just your college.

📊

Progress Tracking

Visual dashboards show your learning journey. Track sessions, skills mastered, and reputation score over time.

🔒

Safe & Verified Users

College email verification ensures everyone on the platform is a genuine student. Safe, trusted, and focused.
